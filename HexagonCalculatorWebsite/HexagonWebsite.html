Hexagon Calculator

Measurement Types

  • Apothem: The line that runs from the center of the hexagon bisecting a flat side.
  • Area: The area of the hexagon.
  • Center to Point: The line that runs from thhe center of the hexagon to a vertex.
  • Flat to Flat: The line that runs from one flat side to its opposite flat side.
  • Perimeter: The perimeter of the hexagon.
  • Point to Point: The line that runs from one vertex to its opposite vertex.
  • Side: A flat side of the hex. This is also congruent with the "Center to Point" measurement.

Select which measurement type you have to work with from the drop down list, input the value into the text box, and press calculate. The corresponding measurements will be produced.
