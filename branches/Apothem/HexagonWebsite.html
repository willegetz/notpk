Hexagon Calculator

Measurement Types

  • Apothem: The line that runs from the center of the hexagon bisecting a flat side.
  • Area: The measurement of the surface of the hexagon.
  • Center to Vertex: The line that runs from the center of the hexagon to a vertex (or corner).
  • Side to Side: The line that runs from one flat side to its opposite flat side.
  • Perimeter: The distance around the outside of the hexagon.
  • Vertex to Vertex: The line that runs from one vertex (or corner) to its opposite vertex (or corner).
  • Side: A flat side of the hex. This is also congruent with the "Center to Vertex" measurement.

Select which measurement type you have to work with from the drop down list, input the value into the text box, and press calculate. The corresponding measurements will be produced.
